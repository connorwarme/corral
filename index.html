Corral Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 20, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Decode Solutions LLC ("we," "us," or "our") handles information in connection with your use of the Corral mobile application (the "App").

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

Your privacy is important to us. Corral is designed to provide users with a seamless way to organize and share trips, as well as find nearby friends and recommended events. We are committed to protecting the limited information we collect and ensuring transparency about our practices.

Information We Collect

We want to be clear: Corral does not collect any personal data from you beyond what you explicitly provide to us through the App's functionalities.

The information we collect is directly provided by you when you use specific features within the App:

  • Account Information: When you create an account, you provide us with information such as your name, email address, and password. This information is used solely to create and manage your account and to allow you to log in to the App.

  • Trip/Event Information: When you create, modify, or interact with trips, events, or similar features within the App, you may provide details such as trip names, dates, locations, descriptions, and participant information. This information is stored to enable the App's core functionality for you and your designated participants.

  • User-Generated Content: Any other content you explicitly create or input into the App (e.g., notes, photos, comments) is stored to fulfill the App's functionality.

  • Contact Information (Optional): If you choose to invite friends or share content within the App using your device's contacts, we may temporarily access your contacts only for the purpose of enabling you to select specific contacts for sharing, and we do not store your contact list on our servers.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide exclusively for the following purposes:

  • To Provide and Maintain the App: To operate the App's features, create your account, and manage the content you create (like trips, events, etc.).

  • To Enable App Functionality: To allow you to interact with other users if the App supports multi-user features (e.g., sharing trips with friends you invite).

  • For Communication Related to the App: To send you essential service-related communications, such as password reset instructions or critical updates about the App.

  • For Internal Analytics and Improvement: We may use aggregated, anonymized, or de-identified data (which cannot be linked back to you) for internal analysis to understand how our App is used and to improve its features and performance.

How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information with third parties.

Your information may be shared in limited circumstances:

  • With Other Users (at your direction): If you use App features that involve sharing information with other users (e.g., inviting friends to a trip), the information you choose to share will be accessible to those users.

  • With Third-Party Service Providers: We may use third-party service providers to facilitate our App's operation (e.g., hosting providers, database services). These providers are contractually obligated to protect your information and use it only for the purposes for which we disclose it to them.

  • For Legal Reasons: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to comply with legal obligations, protect and defend our rights or property, or prevent fraud or illegal activity.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers maintained by our third-party hosting provider AWS. We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. Therefore, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Your Choices and Rights

  • Account Information: You can access, update, or delete your account information directly within the App's settings or by contacting support at the email below.

  • Data Deletion: If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, please contact support at the email below. We will process your request within a reasonable timeframe.

  • Opt-Out: You can opt-out of receiving non-essential communications from us by following the unsubscribe instructions in those communications.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
